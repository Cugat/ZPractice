Felicia Siegrist

In the year of 1991, our hero, Felicia Siegrist was born in Rupperswil, a small town between Zürich and Basel, Switzerland. Since very early, she showed some glimpses of her genius. Always a leader and a brillant student, she was voted the best student in the history of her school in Aarau, the same school Albert Einstein studied. She kept learning through the years and the only dumb movement in her whole life, was date José "Pandita" Pérez-Hita.
